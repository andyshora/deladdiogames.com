De Laddio Free Kicks

De Laddio Free Kicks is here to take you back to the glory days of arcade-style football games! None of this swiping/flicking nonsense, simply set the direction power and curve of your free kicks and deposit your goals into the back of the onion sack.

  • SCORE GOALS AND WIN CUPS!
    Every now and again the football gods will present you with a rare opportunity to win some silverware! Very much a gauge of where you are in the global football ladder, your goal:cup ratio is something to be proud of.
  • USE YOUR TEAMMATES
    Put one of your teammates in a position to shoot, and they'll do their best to outmuscle the defenders and finish off the move!
  • OVER 30 LEVELS
    De Laddio Free Kicks debuts with over 30 different free kick scenarios to keep you entertained for hours. Featuring 4 pitches, 10 opposition teams, and loads of updates coming very soon!
