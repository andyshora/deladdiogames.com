De Laddio Free Kicks

Remember the 90s, when proper arcade style football games nailed the gameplay? Well, De Laddio Free Kicks will rekindle those feelings!

  • Shoot your way to glory by specifying direction, power and curl.
  • Swing the ball over to your attacking-minded teammates, and they'll attempt to bury it in the onion sack!
  • Score goals, and wait for magic chances to win cups!
